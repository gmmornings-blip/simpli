# FOUNDERS.md

> **The Founder’s Letter**
>
> *Written at the beginning of the journey.*
>
> *Version 0.1.0*

---

# Why We Started

Modern technology promised to make life simpler.

In many ways, it has.

But it has also become fragmented.

Today, people manage their lives across dozens of disconnected applications.

One app remembers appointments.

Another stores notes.

Another tracks expenses.

Another manages work.

Another plans meals.

Another monitors health.

Each solves a small problem.

Few work together.

As a result, people spend more time managing software than benefiting from it.

Simpli exists because we believe technology can do better.

---

# Our Belief

Life is connected.

Software should be too.

A grocery purchase affects a budget.

A budget influences goals.

Goals shape priorities.

Priorities determine tasks.

Tasks fill calendars.

Calendars affect health.

Health impacts productivity.

Everything connects.

We believe software should understand these relationships instead of forcing people to recreate them across multiple applications.

---

# What We Are Building

We are not building another productivity app.

We are building a platform.

A personal operating system.

A place where information is entered once, connected intelligently, and reused wherever it creates value.

The goal is not to make people spend more time inside Simpli.

The goal is to help them spend less time managing life and more time living it.

---

# The Kind of Company We Want to Be

We measure success differently.

Success is not the number of features.

Success is not the number of notifications sent.

Success is not the amount of time users spend staring at screens.

Success is measured by questions like:

* Did we save someone time?
* Did we reduce unnecessary effort?
* Did we remove confusion?
* Did we respect people's attention?
* Did we build something we would proudly recommend to our own families?

If the answer is yes, we are moving in the right direction.

---

# Our Commitment

We will choose simplicity over complexity.

We will choose openness over secrecy whenever possible.

We will design for people, not algorithms.

We will build for the long term rather than chasing short-term trends.

We will document our thinking so others can build upon it.

We will welcome contributors who share these values.

---

# Our Relationship with Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence is a powerful tool.

It is not the purpose of Simpli.

We will use AI where it genuinely reduces repetitive work, improves understanding, or helps people make better decisions.

We will not add AI simply because it is fashionable.

Users should always understand when AI is involved and remain in control of important decisions.

---

# Our Relationship with Data

People trust us with information that represents their lives.

That trust is earned—not assumed.

We believe users own their data.

We will strive to make information portable, transparent, and secure.

Privacy is not an optional feature to be added later.

It is part of the foundation.

---

# Building in the Open

Simpli is an open-source project because we believe good ideas become better when they are shared.

Open source is more than a license.

It is an invitation to collaborate, learn, question, improve, and teach.

Every contributor leaves the project better than they found it.

Every improvement benefits the community.

---

# A Promise to Future Contributors

If you are reading this months or years after this project began, welcome.

Whether you are fixing a typo, translating documentation, improving accessibility, writing tests, or designing a major feature, your work matters.

We ask only one thing:

Respect the principles that brought Simpli into existence.

Improve the platform without making it more complicated.

Leave room for those who come after you.

Build software you would be proud to maintain.

---

# A Promise to Ourselves

There will be moments when progress feels slow.

There will be features that need to be rewritten.

There will be ideas that fail.

There will be times when it feels easier to compromise.

When those moments arrive, we return to our purpose.

We are not here to build the biggest software platform.

We are here to build one of the clearest.

One thoughtful decision.

One meaningful feature.

One commit at a time.

---

# The Question That Guides Us

Before every major decision, ask:

> **Does this genuinely make life simpler?**

If the answer is yes, continue.

If the answer is no, rethink the solution.

---

# Our North Star

We believe the best technology quietly disappears into the background.

It should organize, connect, and assist without demanding constant attention.

Our ambition is not to become indispensable because people cannot live without Simpli.

Our ambition is to become trusted because people know Simpli helps them live with greater clarity, confidence, and peace of mind.

---

# Final Words

Every project begins with an idea.

Few become something people rely on.

The difference is rarely talent alone.

It is patience.

Discipline.

Curiosity.

Craftsmanship.

And the willingness to keep building when progress is measured in small, quiet steps.

This document marks the beginning of that journey.

May every future version of Simpli remain worthy of the trust placed in it.
